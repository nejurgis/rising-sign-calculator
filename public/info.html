What is a Rising Sign?

The rising sign, also known as the Ascendant, is the zodiac sign that was ascending on the eastern horizon at the specific time and location of your birth.

In astrology, it represents the "mask" you wear when meeting others, your outward personality, and your physical appearance. While your Sun sign represents your inner core and ego, your Rising sign is how you interface with the world.

Because the Earth rotates, the rising sign changes approximately every two hours. This is why having an accurate birth time is crucial for calculating your precise astrological chart.
