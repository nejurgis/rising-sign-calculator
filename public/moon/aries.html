Aries

Moon Sign

Your emotional world moves fast. You feel things first and process them later — instinct over reflection, action over sitting with it. When something excites you, it consumes you. When something hurts you, you need to move, not stew. There's a refreshing directness to your feelings: you're rarely passive-aggressive, rarely sulking in silence for long.

What Aries Moon needs

Independence and the freedom to act on impulse. You feel most emotionally grounded when you're moving toward something — a goal, a project, a new start. Relationships work best when your autonomy is respected and your energy has somewhere to go.
