Cancer

Moon Sign

The Moon is at home in Cancer, and you carry that everywhere. Your emotional antenna is finely tuned — you pick up on moods, undercurrents, and what isn't being said. You feel things deeply and for a long time. Home isn't just a place for you; it's the emotional anchor you return to, literally or in your mind, when the world gets to be too much.

What Cancer Moon needs

Safety, belonging, and people you can trust completely. You give a great deal emotionally, and you need to know that generosity is matched. When you feel at home — in a place, a relationship, a community — you flourish. When you don't, you retreat, and that withdrawal is your most honest signal.
