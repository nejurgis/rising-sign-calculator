Capricorn

Moon Sign

You don't lead with your feelings, and you're quietly proud of that. There's a seriousness to your emotional world — you have high standards for yourself, and you extend those standards to your inner life too. Vulnerability feels like exposure; need feels like weakness. What drives you runs deep, and much of it doesn't make it to the surface. The work is in learning that needing people is not the same as losing ground.

What Capricorn Moon needs

Structure, achievement, and respect. You feel most settled when you're making progress toward something that matters. Emotional security for you is built on the solid ground of your own competence — but the people who get past your reserve will find more warmth than they expected.
