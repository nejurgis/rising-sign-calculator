Gemini

Moon Sign

Your feelings arrive in language. You process emotion by talking about it — or writing, thinking, running the scenario from every angle. What looks like overthinking from the outside is actually how you make sense of your inner life. You need mental stimulation the way others need physical rest; without enough variety and exchange, your mood quietly dips.

What Gemini Moon needs

Conversation and change of scene. You feel most yourself when there's something new to learn or someone interesting to talk with. Emotional depth comes naturally — but only once your mind has had room to roam. Give yourself permission to need mental engagement as much as warmth.
