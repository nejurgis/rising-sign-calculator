Leo

Moon Sign

Your emotional world is large and warm. You feel things fully and aren't particularly interested in hiding it — suppressing your enthusiasm feels unnatural, almost dishonest. You're generous with love and attention, and you respond to warmth with warmth. Where you stumble is when your feelings are overlooked or dismissed; you need to be seen, not merely tolerated.

What Leo Moon needs

Recognition and genuine appreciation. Not flattery — you can tell the difference — but honest acknowledgment that you matter. When people show up for you the way you show up for them, you give everything in return. The risk is tying your emotional stability too tightly to others' response.
