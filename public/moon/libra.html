Libra

Moon Sign

Your emotional wellbeing is tied to your relationships. When things are harmonious — when there's ease and fairness in your close connections — you feel genuinely well. Conflict and discord are not just unpleasant; they're destabilising. This sensitivity to others can make you an exceptional companion and a skilled peacemaker, though it sometimes costs you access to your own unfiltered feelings.

What Libra Moon needs

Balance and partnership. You think and feel better in dialogue with someone you respect. Solitude can be useful in small doses, but you find your emotional footing in relation to others. Learning to know your own position before asking for theirs is the deeper work.
