Sagittarius

Moon Sign

Your emotional life craves horizon. You feel most yourself when there's somewhere to go — literally or intellectually — and least yourself when you're confined, obligated, or stuck. You have a natural optimism that can lift the mood of a room, and a tendency to intellectualise your feelings as a way of making them lighter. The deeper work for Sagittarius Moon is learning to stay present when the urge to move on is strong.

What Sagittarius Moon needs

Freedom and meaning. You need to believe there's a bigger picture. Travel, learning, philosophy, adventure — these aren't indulgences for you; they're emotional sustenance. Relationships that try to contain you will always lose to the ones that make room for you to roam.
