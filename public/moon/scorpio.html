Scorpio

Moon Sign

Your emotions run deeper than most people suspect. You rarely share what you feel until you're certain it's safe — which means most people never see the full picture. What they do see is intensity: you don't do casual. You feel things with a permanence that lighter signs find hard to understand, and you remember. Betrayal registers differently for you than for most.

What Scorpio Moon needs

Total loyalty and earned trust. You open slowly, but once you do, you give completely. The people in your inner circle experience a depth of devotion that's genuinely rare. You need relationships that can hold your full emotional weight — and you need to know the door only goes one way.
