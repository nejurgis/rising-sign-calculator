Taurus

Moon Sign

Your emotional life is rooted in the physical world. You feel secure through consistency — familiar places, familiar people, the same comforts you've known to work. You don't trust feelings that arrive in a rush; you need time to let things settle before you know what you actually think. Your steadiness makes you a calming presence, though once you've decided something, changing course takes real effort.

What Taurus Moon needs

Stability, beauty, and time. You're restored by simple pleasures — good food, quiet evenings, the tactile world. When routines are disrupted, you feel it as genuine stress. Security isn't a luxury for you; it's a requirement, and the people closest to you will come to rely on your unshakeable presence.
