Virgo

Moon Sign

You process emotions through analysis. When something troubles you, you pull it apart, look for the pattern, try to understand what went wrong and how to fix it. This makes you extraordinarily perceptive and genuinely useful to the people you care about — you notice what needs doing and quietly do it. The challenge is learning when something doesn't need fixing, only feeling.

What Virgo Moon needs

Usefulness and order. You feel emotionally settled when your environment is organised and your skills are being put to good use. Serving the people you love is one of the most natural expressions of your affection — but the care you extend to others is also owed to yourself.
